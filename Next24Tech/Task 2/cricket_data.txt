What is cricket? Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players.
Who is Sachin Tendulkar? Sachin Tendulkar is a former Indian cricketer widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket.
Who won the ICC Cricket World Cup in 2019? England won the ICC Cricket World Cup in 2019.
What are the rules of cricket? Cricket is played on a rectangular field called a pitch, with a wicket (composed of three stumps and two bails) at each end. The objective is for one team to score more runs than their opponent.
What is a century in cricket? A century in cricket refers to a batsman scoring 100 or more runs in a single innings.
Who is known as the 'God of Cricket'? Sachin Tendulkar is often referred to as the 'God of Cricket'.
When was the first cricket match played? The first officially recognized cricket match took place in 1646 in England.
Who is the leading run-scorer in international cricket? Sachin Tendulkar holds the record for the most runs scored in international cricket.
What are the different formats of cricket? The main formats of cricket are Test cricket, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20) cricket.
Who is the current captain of the Indian cricket team? As of 2024, Virat Kohli is the captain of the Indian cricket team.
Who invented cricket? Cricket's origins date back to the late 16th century, with its modern form originating in England.
What is the Ashes series in cricket? The Ashes is a Test cricket series played between England and Australia.
What is the Duckworth-Lewis method in cricket? The Duckworth-Lewis method is a mathematical formula used to calculate target scores in limited-overs cricket matches affected by weather conditions.
Who has the highest individual score in Test cricket? Brian Lara from the West Indies holds the record for the highest individual score in Test cricket, scoring 400 not out.
What is a hat-trick in cricket? A hat-trick in cricket refers to a bowler taking three wickets on consecutive deliveries in the same match.
Who has the most international centuries in cricket? Sachin Tendulkar holds the record for the most international centuries, with 100 centuries across Test and One Day International (ODI) cricket.
What is the IPL in cricket? The IPL (Indian Premier League) is a professional Twenty20 (T20) cricket league in India, contested annually by franchise teams representing different cities.
Who is the leading wicket-taker in international cricket? Muttiah Muralitharan from Sri Lanka holds the record for the most wickets taken in international cricket.
What is a yorker in cricket? A yorker in cricket is a delivery bowled with the intention of pitching the ball right at the batsman's feet, making it difficult to hit.
Who is the fastest bowler in cricket history? Shoaib Akhtar from Pakistan is widely considered one of the fastest bowlers in cricket history, having bowled deliveries exceeding 100 miles per hour.
What is the ICC Champions Trophy in cricket? The ICC Champions Trophy was a One Day International (ODI) cricket tournament held every four years featuring the top cricketing nations.
Who won the ICC Cricket World Cup in 2023? The ICC Cricket World Cup in 2023 was won by England.
Who is known as the 'Master Blaster' in cricket? Vivian Richards from the West Indies is often referred to as the 'Master Blaster' for his aggressive batting style.
What is a no-ball in cricket? A no-ball in cricket is an illegal delivery by the bowler that results in the batting team being awarded an additional run and any runs scored off that delivery.
Who is the youngest cricketer to score a century in Test cricket? Mohammad Ashraful from Bangladesh is the youngest cricketer to score a century in Test cricket, achieving this feat at the age of 17.
What is the ICC ODI World Cup? The ICC ODI World Cup is a One Day International (ODI) cricket tournament held every four years, featuring national teams from ICC member countries.
Who has won the most ICC Cricket World Cup titles? Australia holds the record for the most ICC Cricket World Cup titles, having won the tournament five times.
What is the ICC T20 World Cup? The ICC T20 World Cup is an international Twenty20 cricket tournament held every two years, featuring national teams from ICC member countries.
Who is the highest run-scorer in IPL history? Virat Kohli holds the record for the highest run-scorer in IPL history.
What is a googly in cricket? A googly is a type of delivery bowled by a leg-spinner that spins in the opposite direction to their stock delivery, deceiving the batsman.
Who is the fastest batsman to reach 10,000 runs in One Day Internationals (ODIs)? Virat Kohli from India is the fastest batsman to reach 10,000 runs in ODIs, achieving this milestone in just 205 innings.
What is the DRS (Decision Review System) in cricket? The DRS is a technology-based system used in cricket to assist the on-field umpires in making decisions regarding disputed calls such as LBW (Leg Before Wicket) and catches.
Who holds the record for the most sixes in international cricket? Shahid Afridi from Pakistan holds the record for hitting the most sixes in international cricket.
What is the Mankad dismissal in cricket? The Mankad dismissal, named after Indian cricketer Vinoo Mankad, refers to a method of run out where the bowler runs out the non-striking batsman before delivering the ball if the batsman leaves the crease too early.
Who is the leading century-maker in Test cricket? Sachin Tendulkar holds the record for the most centuries scored in Test cricket, with 51 centuries.
What is a bouncer in cricket? A bouncer is a short-pitched delivery bowled by a fast bowler with the intention of rising towards the batsman's head or upper body, often used as a tactic to intimidate or force a defensive stroke.
Who is the current ICC Test Player of the Year? As of 2024, Kane Williamson from New Zealand is the current ICC Test Player of the Year.
What is the boundary in cricket? The boundary in cricket refers to the perimeter line around the field, marking the edge beyond which a ball hit by the batsman scores four runs (if it touches the ground before reaching the boundary) or six runs (if it clears the boundary on the full).
Who has the most dismissals as a wicketkeeper in international cricket? MS Dhoni from India holds the record for the most dismissals by a wicketkeeper in international cricket, combining catches and stumpings.
What is the ICC Women's T20 World Cup? The ICC Women's T20 World Cup is an international Twenty20 cricket tournament held every two years for women's national teams.
Who is the highest wicket-taker in IPL history? Lasith Malinga from Sri Lanka holds the record for the highest wicket-taker in IPL history.
What is the LBW (Leg Before Wicket) rule in cricket? The LBW rule in cricket states that a batsman can be given out if the ball strikes any part of their body, excluding the hand, before hitting the bat and the umpire deems that the ball would have gone on to hit the stumps.
Who won the ICC Women's Cricket World Cup in 2022? Australia won the ICC Women's Cricket World Cup in 2022.
What is the ICC Associate Member in cricket? The ICC Associate Members are cricket-playing nations that are not Full Members of the International Cricket Council (ICC) but have been recognized for their development of the sport.
